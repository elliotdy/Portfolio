Balancing Car

A self-balancing motor wheel control project focused on chassis layout, wheel design, support components, hardware integration, physical prototyping, and real-world testing. This project combined CAD development with hands-on assembly and performance evaluation.

SolidWorks Mechanical Design Prototyping Testing Assembly Motor Control

Project Overview

This project centered on a compact balancing car platform with custom wheel geometry, support parts, and a lightweight frame arrangement. The work involved CAD modeling, component iteration, physical assembly, and testing of the drive system and overall vehicle behavior.

What I Worked On

  • Modeled the car frame, wheels, spacer, and axle mount components
  • Created top, front, and assembled CAD views to refine layout and fit
  • Built and tested the physical prototype to evaluate motion and control
  • Compared the CAD concept to real fabrication and final assembly
